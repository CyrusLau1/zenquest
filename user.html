ZenQuest Guide

Quest System

Daily Quests

Complete daily tasks to earn XP and maintain your productivity streak. Complete 5+ daily quests to earn bonus rewards and avoid HP penalties.

Habits

Positive Habits: Earn 5 XP and potential Zen Coins when completed.

Negative Habits: Lose 15 HP when marked (use for bad habits you want to avoid).

Main & Side Quests

Long-term objectives that give 15 XP and potential Zen Coins when completed. Set due dates to stay organized.

Focus System

Pomodoro Timer

Focus Sessions (25 min): Earn 50 XP and potential Zen Coins when completed.

Short Break (5 min): Rest period between focus sessions.

Long Break (15 min): Extended rest after multiple focus sessions.

Track your total focus time and session count to monitor productivity.

RPG Mechanics

Experience & Leveling

Gain XP from completing quests and focus sessions. Level up to unlock higher tier equipment and increase Zen Coin rewards.

Health System

Maintain your health by completing daily quests. Failing to complete 5+ daily quests will result in HP penalties. Death causes level loss and coin penalties.

Critical Hits

Equipment can provide critical hit chances, doubling XP and Zen Coin rewards from activities (except XP potions).

Ultimate Goal: Ascend to become the ultimate Zen Master! Reach the highest level, amass legendary Zen Coins, and unlock the most powerful equipment.

Market & Equipment

Equipment System

Owned Stats: Permanent bonuses that scale with item level.

Equipped Stats: Active bonuses only when the item is equipped.

Items can be upgraded by purchasing multiple times, but equipment level is capped by your character level.

Potions

Health Potions: Restore HP instantly.

XP Potions: Grant XP (affected by XP gain bonuses, but no critical hits).

Max HP Potions: Permanently increase maximum health.

Boost Potions: Temporary XP or Coin gain increases.

Tips & Strategies

Optimization Tips

• Focus on completing 5+ daily quests every day for bonus rewards

• Use the Pomodoro timer for deep work sessions

• Invest in equipment that matches your playstyle (XP vs Coin focus)

• Level up equipment gradually as your character level increases

• Set realistic main quest due dates to avoid overwhelming yourself

• Use negative habits sparingly for accountability on bad behaviors

• Save Zen Coins for higher-tier equipment that provides better bonuses
